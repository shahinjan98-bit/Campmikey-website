Welcome to Camp Mikey

Camp Mikey is a premium doggie daycare created to give dogs the perfect balance of fun, exercise and relaxation.

Our days are carefully structured around supervised play, positive socialisation, rest periods and calm downtime, helping dogs enjoy a happy and balanced day.

The Camp Mikey Dog Bus

Our dog school bus style collection service operates in Bray. Dogs are collected in the morning, enjoy their day at Camp Mikey, and are safely returned home.

Half Day

Collection in the morning with return home at 1pm.

Full Day

A full day of daycare with return home at 5pm.
