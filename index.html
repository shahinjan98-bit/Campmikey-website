Welcome to Camp Mikey

Camp Mikey is a premium doggie daycare designed to give dogs an exciting, fulfilling and balanced day. Our dogs enjoy a carefully structured routine combining supervised play, socialisation, rest and calm downtime.

Located in Enniskerry, Wicklow, we provide a safe and enjoyable environment where dogs can have fun while their owners are at work or away for the day.

Our Dog School Bus Service

We offer a dog school bus style collection service in Bray. Dogs are collected in the morning, spend the day at Camp Mikey, and are safely returned home.

Half Day

Collection in the morning with return home at 1pm.

Full Day

A full day of fun, enrichment, socialisation and relaxation with return home at 5pm.
