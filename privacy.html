Privacy Policy

About This Policy

Camp Mikey respects your privacy and takes the protection of your personal information seriously. This policy explains what information we collect through our website, why we collect it and how we use and store it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a Trial Day request, we collect information you provide about yourself and your dog. This may include your name, phone number, home address, your dog's details, veterinary practice, vaccination status, temperament, behaviour and social experience.

Why We Collect Your Information

We use this information to respond to your Trial Day request, arrange your dog's trial day, assess their individual needs and help provide a safe environment for all dogs attending Camp Mikey.

How Your Information Is Stored

Trial Day requests submitted through our website are stored securely in our website database. A copy of the enquiry is also sent to our Camp Mikey business email account so that we can respond to you.

How Long We Keep Your Information

If your enquiry does not result in your dog becoming a Camp Mikey customer, we aim to delete the information provided through your Trial Day request within six months.

If your dog becomes a Camp Mikey customer, relevant information may be retained for as long as it is reasonably required to provide our services and manage our relationship with you.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information is only shared where necessary to operate our services, comply with a legal obligation, or protect the safety and welfare of the dogs in our care.

Your Data Protection Rights

You may have rights under applicable data protection law, including the right to request access to your personal information, ask for inaccurate information to be corrected, and request deletion of your information in certain circumstances.
